Welcome to Punk Voyage Adventures!

Objective: Send your adventurers on exciting quests to earn PV Tokens and Gold.

How to Play:

  • Connect your wallet to manage your game assets (future feature).
  • Use the "ADVENTURES" page to select an adventure.
  • Choose the number of adventurers to send (currently assumed you have 5).
  • Click "SEND" to start an adventure.
  • Track your active adventures and their return times.
  • Complete quests from the "QUESTS" page for extra rewards.
  • Visit the "SHOP" to spend your PV Tokens on useful items.
  • Don't forget your "DAILY LOGIN REWARDS"!

Dark Mode: Use the toggle in the header to switch between light and dark themes.

This is a demo version. Some features like wallet integration for adventurer NFTs and persistent storage are conceptual.
